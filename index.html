Discover Crete


Crete is the largest and most populous island of Greece. Located on the southern bound of Greece. Crete is separated into four regions called, Heraklion, Lasithi,Rethymno and Chania. The total population of Crete stands at 634.930 people and 160 miles coastline, which is expanding from the western portion of the island to the eastern, while its surrounded from Aegean sea.
The larger island of Greece. Crete is an ark of rare natural beauty, Mediterranean civilization and history. Whoever steps on this land is blessed with mysterious power, warmth , goodness spreaded throughout his veins and his soul grow" says Nikos Kazantzakis the great Cretan author in his book "Reference to Greco".

The most seductive beaches in Crete


You will find the most seductive beaches. One of them is the beach of Balos,following by the Elafonisi lagoon which are considered to be one of the magnificent beaches in the Mediterranean. The biggest beach in Crete is located out of Rethymno city with length 12km. In south singles out the exotic beach of Preveli with the huge dunes. In the region of Herakleion, the most famous destinations are Malia and Peninsula. You will find thousands of kilometres of sand around the Ierapetra village in region of Lasithi and the famous beach of Vai with the exotic palm-forest.

Genuine hospitality that remains unforgettable


The genuine, majestic and generous Cretan landscape has left its imprints on the Cretan spirit and the mentality of the people. The soul of the locals seems to follow step by step the authenticity and grandeur of the landscape. Families are ready to offer you vegetables from the garden.Owners of traditional cafes are willing to offer you (Dako and raki) Cretan products and will share beautiful life stories, fatal vendettas and valuable values such as love, honour, respect, family and friendships.

Discover paradise


Crete is a unique destination for all kinds of sports and activities while the most famous its gorges and natural landscapes. On the top of the list stands the Samaria Gorge. Also, you can enjoy escapes by 4x4 cars as the Crete is full of raw roads where you can see unique images such as mountains, plateaus and pristine seas with incredible colours and views.
The homeland of the Mediterranean diet

Crete is the mother of flavours. Cretan diet contains a rusk salad with coarse salt, oil, olives, feta cheese and artichokes called (Dakos). Discover the secrets of Cretan cuisine, one of the most healthy diets. Famous Cretan products are the Cretan gruyere, wild greens, wild radishes, handmade pies and fried cheese pies with honey.
